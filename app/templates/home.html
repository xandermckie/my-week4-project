{# ── How to use ── #}

Getting started

How to use Ratio

Tutor Chat

Open the Tutor tab and paste any LSAT question including the stimulus, question stem, and answer choices. Ratio will identify the question type, walk through the reasoning strategy step by step, flag the common trap, and give you a memory cue to recognize the pattern next time. You can ask follow-ups like "explain that more simply" or "why is C wrong?"

Practice Quiz

Go to Quiz for timed practice across Weaken, Assumption, Strengthen, Flaw, Inference, Main Point, Parallel Reasoning, Principle, and Reading Comprehension questions. After submitting your answer you get a full explanation. Every wrong answer is logged to your weak area tracker automatically.

Analysis Dashboard

Check Analysis to see a ranked breakdown of which question types you miss most. Use this to decide what to focus on in your next session, based on real data from your actual practice history rather than gut feeling.

Study Plan

Enter your exam date in Study Plan and Ratio generates a week-by-week schedule that prioritizes your weakest areas. You get one plan per exam window and one free date correction, so set it when you are ready.
